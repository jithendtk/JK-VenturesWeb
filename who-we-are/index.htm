Who We Are

Since our start in 1978, JK Ventures has a proven track record of stability and growth within the private label packaging industry.

Our Story

JK Ventures is a leader in the folding carton manufacturing space with a rich tradition of manufacturing excellence. Our packaging company has serviced customers across the U.S. in providing a broad range of packaging services that improve our client’s speed-to-market, industry presence and profitability margins. The foundation of our success are the people who have come to work at JK Ventures and our amazing long-term client partnerships. We promote a workplace of continuous learning, teamwork, mutual respect and FUN. Located in Methuen, MA we are a short 28 miles outside of Boston.

Watch our video to see why JK Ventures stands out as a company committed to excellence.

Social Responsibility

JK Ventures has its roots as a local company.  We engage with local charities, are a responsible member of our community, and strive to  provide an environment where our employees can grow and thrive, as family.  Additionally we view our environmental responsibility to be our guiding star.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the preferred partner for enterprising  companies seeking comprehensive packaging solutions that drive efficiency, enhance branding, and optimize sustainability. We are committed to delivering innovative, high-quality packaging products and services that exceed our customers’ expectations, while fostering long-term partnerships built on trust, reliability, and exceptional value.
